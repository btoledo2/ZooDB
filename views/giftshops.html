About Us

Our zoo was founded in 1988 with the sole purpose to protect animals in danger. We have come a long way since then and have helped thousands of animals find a home by partnering with animal shelters and conservationist groups. But none of this would be possible without the help of our members. So to our members we Thank you for allowing us to pursue our mission.
